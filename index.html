About Exit Points

 .
Exit Points is a network of improvising musicians and artists.
We organize onine shows and jams, and help newcomers to learn to how to perform online.
Exit Points is run by Michael Palumbo
Our livestream technician is Gabrielle Couillard
If you're interested in joining us, we use Discord to keep in touch. Come say hello.
